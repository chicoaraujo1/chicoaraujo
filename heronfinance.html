Heron Finance

Launched in 2024, Heron Finance is a Private Credit blockchain-based platform on a mission to make it easy to invest in deals once reserved only for institutions. The blockchain technology enables reduced costs, deal transparency, and the portability and interoperability of investments.

My role was to build the platform from 0-1, creating an engaging onboarding, multi functional in-app experience, developing a Design System and a marketing website.
